Bitquant Research Laboratories is a Hong Kong-based startup focusing on research and development of high-risk/high-reward open source financial technologies. Our goal is to take the most advanced technology from various fields of science including astrophysics, computer science, and mathematical finance and make it useful to individual traders, family offices, and small hedge funds.

In addition to our open source products, we also do quantitative and policy research concerning the latest financial technologies.

Research Projects: * Bittrader - Our current focus is to develop a trading platform for individuals and small institutions that will make allow them to deploy trading technology previously available only to large investment banks. * Bitcoin options - We are currently are researching the valuation of bitcoin options and the legal and regulatory framework which would govern option issuance.
