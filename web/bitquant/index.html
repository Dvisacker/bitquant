Your neighborhood financial technology research lab

Bitquant Research Laboratories is a Hong Kong-based startup focusing on research and development of high-risk/high-reward open source financial technologies, and to do quantitative and policy research to support these technolgies.

We take the most advanced technology from astrophysics, computer science, and mathematical finance and make it useful to individual traders, family offices, and small hedge funds.
