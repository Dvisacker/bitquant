Requests for funding

I spend a lot of time helping small companies in Hong Kong get funding, and I also put some of my own money into small companies in Hong Kong. I do not do equity investments, but rather typically do project based investments of about USD 10K. The typical loan would be for a widget manufacturer to pay for a Chinese factory to produce the product, and would be about a 10% overhead for a loan that lasts for about three months. This gives you an effective annual interest rate of about 40%, and if you can get money from someone other than me, you should. On the other hand, banks won't lend for these projects, and while you could get the money from friends and family, the fact that I'm charging you an high interest rate means that you don't owe me any favors.

I also provide product and trade financing. Typically the way that this works is that the wholesaler wants a product from a Chinese factory. I get paid in the form of bitcoin, and add a markup of 25% and a loan of 10% payable in three months. One reason to go through me is that I can guarantee quality of the goods.
