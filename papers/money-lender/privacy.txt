Privacy Waiver

In the event, that a loan is over 60 (sixty) days delinquent, I/we
hereby authorize the borrower to disclose the amounts and nature of
the loans delinquent for a period of 7 years after the deliquency, to
any person or persons including but not limited publicly posting on
the internet and disclosure on social media, provided that the lender
informs the borrower as to the nature of the disclosure and the
persons to whom the disclosure is made to.

I further agree that the transaction logs containing conversations
between the borrower and lender can be used by either side in order to
resolve disputes concerning the loan.
